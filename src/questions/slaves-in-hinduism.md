# Slaves in Hinduism

Slave trade in Hinduism.

> The testing period for slaves is fifteen days, for women it is one month, for all kinds of seeds it is one day and for iron and clothes it is one day.

It is clear from this mention that in ancient India slaves as well as women were considered commodities for sale and purchase.
